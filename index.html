Product Concept

Awoken

Electrolytes, Hydration and Energy provided through 100% Natural Coconut Water and Minerals

Nothing artificial. Everything essential.

The Problem

A cleaner approach to energy

Awoken is a natural electrolyte energy drink built on coconut water — nature’s original hydration source — and thoughtfully selected minerals that support fluid balance and muscle function.

Instead of overstimulation, SOURCE focuses on replenishment. Instead of masking fatigue, it helps restore what your body has already used.

The Product

Designed for steady, usable energy

Your body produces energy most efficiently when it’s properly hydrated and mineral-balanced. SOURCE works by supporting that process rather than overriding it. Each bottle is designed to help replace key electrolytes lost through sweat, movement, or heat, allowing your body to maintain focus and physical output naturally.

  • Rehydrates efficiently using coconut water
  • Supports muscle and nerve function through electrolytes
  • Avoids the artificial spikes common in stimulant-heavy drinks

Awoken isn’t formulated to overpower your system. It’s designed to work with it.
